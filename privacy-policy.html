Privacy Policy for All-in-One Online Tools

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our tools:

  • Files you upload: For processing through our tools (images, documents, audio files, etc.)
  • Automated data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage data: Which tools you use and when

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our tools and services
  • Process your file conversions and calculations
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Prevent abuse of our services

3. File Handling

For tools that process user-uploaded files:

  • Files are temporarily stored only for processing
  • All uploaded files are automatically deleted after processing
  • We do not access file contents except as needed for the tool's function
  • We recommend removing sensitive information before uploading

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic through Google Analytics
  • Serve relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)

5. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding how visitors use our site
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant advertisements

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfers
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to server logs

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
